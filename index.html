Who Are We

Cardiologists are doctors who specialise in diagnosing, treating and preventing diseases that mainly affect the heart and blood vessels.

What Do We Do

Treat patients by reviewing and understanding their medical backgrounds and examining them. Carry out tests such as echocardiograms and interpret test results to measure how effectively the heart is working, which will help you to decide on the best method of treatment.

Working Hours

Cardiologists often work irregular hours and are frequently on call at the weekend. You need to be flexible as hours can be long.Cardiologists typically spend around 75% of their time per week on direct clinical care.
